Welcome to shop

Welcome to our website Candle Heaven. We are an online candle shop compamy founded in 2023. Through our website you can browse through some of our featured items or order your own personalized scented candle. On our website, you can buy candles that will live up your home, or you can buy candles as gifts for people you care about such as your friends or family. We poured a lot of heart and thought into our company, as we think that candles bring a special feeling and mood inside a household. You choose how you want your candle to look like, from setting the wanted size, having the right color and the right scent for your private atmosphere.

What do we offer?

We offer various different candles in our store, lots of various sizes and lots of various colors. Some of the sizes that our candles can be bought in are large and small. After you choose your wanted size, you can pick the candle colors. The colors that we offer are green and red for small candles and purple and yellow for the large candles. After you choose your wanted colors you can pick from the many of the scents we offer. Some of the scents we have available for our candles are vanilla, cinammon, coconut, rose and other exotic scents. We pour a lot of heart into making these candles so that your selection of your wanted candle can be easily made and for your own benefit.
